About the Longmont Ledger

The Longmont Ledger is a modern digital revival of one of Longmont’s earliest newspapers. We’re an independent online magazine focused on stories that matter to Longmont residents and everyone who feels connected to this corner of Colorado.

Our coverage includes:

  • Local & Colorado news
  • Business spotlights and entrepreneurship
  • Arts & culture
  • Recreation and the outdoors
  • History and photography
  • Health & wellness
  • Senior living
  • Technology

We also provide Longmont-area businesses, nonprofits, and community groups an effective way to reach local readers through sponsored features, advertising, press releases, and community announcements.

Your modern Longmont magazine—independent, curious, and community-driven.
